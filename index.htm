All Filaments are PLA unless specfied otherwise.
PLA is a hobbyist plastic that is easily printed and is quite strong. Its downsides are that it is prone to shattering and Melts in strong sunlight (softens at 60°C).
PETG is a more professional but expensive plastic which has similar properties to PET (The Plastic used in water bottles) Its main strengths are that it is stronger than PLA has a higher softening tempature (85°C) and can be Translucent.
